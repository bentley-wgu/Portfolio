EP (Executive Portfolio) is the roll-up of AP + GP + MP. WP is excluded.
AP = FY26 > Volume Trigger AND velocity > Velocity Trigger (High Volume + High Velocity). GP = FY26 ≤ Volume Trigger AND velocity > Velocity Trigger (Low Volume + High Velocity). MP = FY26 > Volume Trigger AND velocity ≤ Velocity Trigger (High Volume + Low Velocity). WP = FY26 ≤ Volume Trigger AND velocity ≤ Velocity Trigger (Low Volume + Low Velocity).
Velocity = (FY26 - FY25) / FY25. New accounts (FY25 = 0, FY26 > 0) are routed to GP automatically, regardless of volume.
FY27 Target = FY26 × (1 + Growth Rate), rounded to whole numbers.
